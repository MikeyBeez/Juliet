For example, say "Julie Julie list commands"

  • Lookup opens a wikipedia article and reads the summary out loud.
  • For example: "Look up Mickey Mouse."
  • Open website requieres a fully qualified domain name.
  • For example, "Open website google.com."
  • Search opens Google.com and tuns on the microphone.
  • Play music will play two random songs.
  • You can change the number of songs in Juliet.py.
  • By editing the totalsongs to play variable.
  • Click, other, and middle are hands-free mouse clicks.
  • "Other" is the right mouse click.
  • Left and right tile a window.
