Just say "Julie Julie" followed by the command.

Juliet understands all these commands:

  • help
  • list commands
  • new terminal
  • open reddit
  • open youtube
  • open website
  • take dictation
  • open microphone
  • look up
  • play music
  • click
  • other
  • middle
  • left
  • right
  • maximize
  • minimize
  • what's up
  • reboot
  • shutdown
  • stop listening

Instructions:

Lookup will display a wikipedia page on whatever topic you've requested.

For example, say: "Julie Julie, Look up Mickey Mouse."

Lookup reads the summary out loud. Hit Ctrl C in the terminal to stop talking.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ctrl C in the terminal will stop a playing song.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Click, other, and middle will left mouse click, right mouse click, and middle mouse click.

Left and right will tile a window to the left or right.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The other commands should be self explanitory.
